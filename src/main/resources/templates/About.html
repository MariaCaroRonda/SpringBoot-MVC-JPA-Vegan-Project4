About Veganism  

How veganism work.
A vegan diet is plant-based and excludes any food derived from animals, such as meat, poultry, fish, or any processed food that contains ingredients derived from animals, such as honey, gelatine, biscuits, etc. Dairy products are also excluded from a vegan diet because they are also of animal origin, such as milk, cheese, yogurt, etc. Sometimes people confuse the words "vegan" and "vegetarian" and think that they have the same meaning. However, a vegetarian diet differs from a vegan diet in that vegetarians can include dairy products in their diet.
